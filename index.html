About Me

I am an adventurous and ambitious person that has a real passion for outdoor activities and sport. I currently play in both the University of Cape Town's rugby and football internal leagues. I recently taken an interest in surfing, mountain biking and trail running.

I enjoy travelling and experiencing the different aspects of new places and cultures. The drive to experience and seek the unkown has been installed in me by my parents. I had lived in four countries by the age of ten and truly seek to live in as many as I can in the future.

In addition to sport and the outdoors, I value my academic work massively. I was driven to study Geomatics as geography, mathematics and physics were my favourite subjects in school. I have always had the drive to invest as much as I can into my studies and I know this will apply into my working career.

I have always been a person that believes success can be measured by the relationships you have with your friends, family and loved ones.
